Ceaser Salad

Description

A delicious wagyu burger on a brioche bun.

Ingredients

  • Wagyu Patty
  • Brioche Bun
  • Burger Sauce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan.
  2. Cook the burger patty to your desired doneness.
  3. Toast the bun in the pan.
  4. Assemble all the condiments on the bun.
  5. Place the patty on the bun.
  6. Enjoy!
